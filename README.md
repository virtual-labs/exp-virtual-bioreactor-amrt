### About the Experiment 

This is an interactive platform for students to master the instrumentation and control systems essential for microbial or cell cultivation in a laboratory-scale stirred-tank bioreactor. Through the simulation, users can manage critical environmental parameters—including agitation speed, oxygen supply via sparging, temperature regulation, and the monitoring of pH and dissolved oxygen levels—to maintain an optimal environment for biological growth. By integrating bioprocess kinetics and mathematical modeling, the lab allows for the exploration of different cultivation modes, such as batch, fed-batch, and continuous systems, to observe how varying conditions directly impact growth rates and product yields. This virtual environment serves as a vital tool for learning sterile operation, sensor calibration, and process optimization, preparing students for real-world applications in biotechnology.
